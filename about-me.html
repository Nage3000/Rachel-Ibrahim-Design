build & sustain your brand

I've spent the greater part of 10 years trying to understand technology, and before you can blink - it changes! Like technology, we must adjust our habits and acclimate to new trends. It's the only way small businesses can survive in a rich man's world.

I focus on small businesses, helping them not only build or rebuild their brand, but also developing strategies to sustain the brand in this digital world. My foundation began with Marketing and eventually progressed to UX Design and Graphic Design.

Thanks for checking out my page - let's get to work!

"Brand is just a perception, and perception will match reality over time. Sometimes it will be ahead, other times it will be behind. But brand is simply a collective impression some have about a product."
